Project Description

Formula Fighters is an mathematics-based deck building game, designed for late elementary schoolers to enhance their learning development. The player will progress through levels, fighting enemies and progresing through the game. In an individual level, the player and the computer opponent will take turns battling through the attack and defense phases. During the attack phase players construct an equation using operator and integer cards from their hand, using the value evaluated from the equation to attack the enemy. During the defense phase players construct an equation in the same way, using the value evaluated as a defense value against oncoming attacks. As you progress you get valuable loot in the form of cards to add to your deck.
