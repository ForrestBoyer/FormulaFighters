Project Description

Formula Fighters is an mathematics-based deck building game, designed for late elementary schoolers to enhance their learning development. The player will progress through levels, fighting enemies and progressing through the game. In an individual level, the player and the computer opponent will take turns battling through the attack and defense phases. During the attack phase, players construct an equation using operator and integer cards from their hand, using the value evaluated from the equation to attack the enemy. During the defense phase, players construct an equation in the same way, using the value evaluated as a defense value against oncoming attacks. As you progress, you get valuable loot in the form of cards to add to your deck.

Project Background

Formula Fighters is a game designed to bring the worlds of classic deckbuilding games, such as Dominion or Slay the Spire, together with an educational component. The idea is to be both educational and entertaining at the same time, giving users a fun experience while also having them learn. The game aims to be playable with or without prior math skills, with users hopefully picking some up along the way. Due to the level of math involved in the game, the general target audience is late elementary school students, but the game should be enjoyable for all ages.
